Nature Breakout-Clone

  1. The Target Audience is nature lovers.
  2. The game automatically starts when you click on the link from earlier.
  3. The goal is to destroy all the bricks. Use the paddle and arrow keys (left and right) to move the paddle to bounce the ball to destroy the bricks.
  4. If the ball passes over your side, you lose.

Nature Whackamole (Open Source)

  1. The game is pretty simple. There is no time limit but try your best to click on all the moles without dying to the other plants and aliens.
  2. There are three difficulties you can choose from, which i something I implemented and added on top of this open source game. In addition, I added an extra alien that will also end the game

Forester(Platformer)

  1. To play this game, move the knight with the arrow keys and jump. You can only tap them but you have infinite jumps with the space bar. Try to collect all the coins to win the game.
  2. If you get hit by the alien, you lose.
